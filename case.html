LeanOS Case Studies

Small manufacturer

Before and After Workflow

Situation: Customer orders moved through manual handoffs, unclear approvals and repeated status checks.

LeanOS approach: Current-state map, bottleneck review, standard work, daily visibility board and a future-state workflow proposal.

Implementation focus: Ownership rules, queue limits, escalation triggers and simple dashboard measures.

Result to add: Lead time reduction, fewer escalations, clearer accountability and measurable order-flow stability.

Professional services

Waste Removal Sprint

Situation: Employees repeated admin steps across email, spreadsheets and shared folders.

LeanOS approach: TIMWOODS workshop, waste scoring, automation shortlist and a quick implementation plan.

Implementation focus: Intake standardization, duplicate-entry removal and clearer document storage routines.

Result to add: Time saved per week, fewer manual follow-ups and tasks moved from manual to automated.

Healthcare clinic

Quality System Setup

Situation: Rework and inconsistent documentation created delays and made audits harder.

LeanOS approach: Quality assessment toolkit, 7 QC tools, visual error tracking and practical assurance routines.

Implementation focus: Defect categories, standard forms, cause-and-effect review and weekly quality check-ins.

Result to add: Error reduction, faster response time, cleaner audit trail and better team confidence.

Retail or apparel team

Training Transformation

Situation: Improvement ideas existed, but teams lacked a shared method for selecting and testing changes.

LeanOS approach: Lean basics, 5S, waste identification, team workshop activities and idea capture routines.

Implementation focus: Practical exercises, team ownership, visible idea boards and short improvement cycles.

Result to add: Implemented ideas, participation rate, visible workplace improvements and stronger daily habits.
