Case study samples

Four styles that fit LeanOS work

Before and After Workflow

Client type: Small manufacturer

Problem: Manual handoffs and unclear ownership delayed customer orders.

LeanOS approach: Current-state map, bottleneck review, standard work and daily visibility board.

Result to add: Lead time reduction, fewer escalations and clearer accountability.

Waste Removal Sprint

Client type: Professional services team

Problem: Employees repeated admin steps across email, spreadsheets and shared folders.

LeanOS approach: TIMWOODS workshop, automation shortlist and quick implementation plan.

Result to add: Time saved per week and tasks moved from manual to automated.

Quality System Setup

Client type: Small healthcare clinic

Problem: Rework and inconsistent documentation created service delays.

LeanOS approach: Quality assessment toolkit, 7 QC tools and visual error tracking.

Result to add: Error reduction, faster response time and cleaner audit trail.

Training Transformation

Client type: Retail or apparel team

Problem: Improvement ideas existed but teams lacked a shared method.

LeanOS approach: Lean basics, 5S, waste identification and team workshop activities.

Result to add: Implemented ideas, participation rate and visible team improvements.
