Brand template

LeanOS Consultants color, font and content style guide.

A practical reference for future blogs, posts, course materials, proposals and emails.

Color palette

Deep teal - #075E67
Primary brand color. Use about 45%.

Medium teal - #2CA6A4
Support color for tags, highlights and subtle sections. Use about 20%.

Orange - #F47C20
CTA and energy color. Use about 10%.

Light background - #F4F8F8
Quiet page sections. Use about 15%.

Ink - #21313C
Body text and headings when teal is too strong. Use about 10%.

Typography

Primary font: Open Sans, Calibri, Arial, sans-serif.

Why: Open Sans gives the site a modern professional services feel, while Calibri and Arial keep the fallback clean and readable.

Heading style: Clear, direct and outcome-led. Use sentence case.

Body style: Practical, confident and specific. Prefer concrete workflow language over broad consulting language.

CTA style: Use direct action labels such as Book a Strategy Call, Get a Proposal, Request an Assessment and Enroll in a Course.

Visual use

  • Use the logo on white or very light backgrounds where possible.
  • Keep orange for arrows, buttons, key numbers and next actions.
  • Use teal for trust, structure, frameworks and headings.
  • Avoid using only teal; balance with white space, ink text and restrained orange.

Message pillars

  • Automate daily workflows.
  • Reduce hidden waste and lead time.
  • Build quality systems employees can run.
  • Teach lean skills through courses and workshops.
  • Use AI agents where they make work faster and clearer.
